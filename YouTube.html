The History Of YouTube

  • YouTube is a app that is used regularly by the citizens of the worls. But its history is still to be advertised and shown to the world.
  • It was registered on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim, three former employees of the American e-commerce company PayPal.
  • They had the idea that ordinary people would enjoy sharing their “home videos.” The company is headquartered in San Bruno, California.
